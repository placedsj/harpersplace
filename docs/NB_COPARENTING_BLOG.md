# Content Strategy: "The New Brunswick Blueprint" Blog

## Mission
To establish Harper's Place as the leading authority on child-centered co-parenting technology in New Brunswick by providing actionable, legally-informed content that helps parents navigate separation and fulfill their obligations under the Divorce Act.

## Target Audience
- Parents in New Brunswick currently undergoing or anticipating separation/divorce.
- Family law professionals (lawyers, mediators, judges) in New Brunswick looking for client resources.
- UNB Legal Innovation Lab stakeholders.

## Core Themes
1.  **Child-First Communication:** Practical guides for conflict-free interaction.
2.  **Financial Transparency:** Demystifying Section 7 expenses and child support in NB.
3.  **Court-Ready Documentation:** Best practices for creating admissible evidence.
4.  **Navigating the NB Family Justice System:** Translating legal jargon into plain language.

## Proposed Blog Posts

### Post 1: The "I Statement" Advantage
- **Title:** The "I Statement" Advantage: Fulfilling the Duty to Protect Children from Parental Conflict
- **NB Law Connection:** Directly addresses the **Divorce Act's s. 7.1** duty by teaching conflict-free communication.
- **Harper's Place Solution:** Showcase the **AI Communication Coach** by showing before-and-after examples.
  - *Before (Hostile):* "Why are you always late?"
  - *After (AI-Revised):* "I get worried when the schedule isn't met. Can we confirm a pickup time that works for you?"
- **Call to Action:** "Try our AI Coach to rephrase your difficult conversations."

### Post 2: Demystifying Section 7 Expenses
- **Title:** Stop Fighting About Who Pays: Navigating Section 7 Expenses in New Brunswick
- **NB Law Connection:** Focuses on *extraordinary expenses* (e.g., hockey, tutoring) and the legal requirement for clear communication and documentation.
- **Harper's Place Solution:** Feature **Harper's Fund**. Show how to log an expense, upload a receipt, categorize it via AI (e.g., "Extracurricular"), and export a report for court or personal records.
- **Call to Action:** "Track every child-related expense transparently with Harper's Fund."

### Post 3: The Power of Timestamps
- **Title:** Why Judges Love Timestamps: Making Your Records Court-Admissible in NB
- **NB Law Connection:** Explains the shift to electronic filing in New Brunswick and the necessity of verifiable, timestamped evidence over "he said, she said" arguments.
- **Harper's Place Solution:** Highlight the **Evidence Log**. Explain that every entry is automatically timestamped and cannot be altered, providing a reliable record for legal proceedings.
- **Call to Action:** "Start building your court-ready record today with our secure Evidence Log."

### Post 4: Parenting Time vs. Decision-Making
- **Title:** Beyond the Schedule: Understanding Parenting Time vs. Decision-Making Responsibility in NB
- **NB Law Connection:** Clarifies the new terminology in the **Divorce Act**, helping parents focus on the child's needs rather than getting stuck on legal jargon.
- **Harper's Place Solution:** Show how the **Smart Calendar** tracks parenting time while the **Daily Care Log** (noting moods, health) and **Health Hub** (tracking doctor's appointments) can inform decision-making responsibilities.
- **Call to Action:** "Manage both your schedule and your child's well-being with our integrated tools."
